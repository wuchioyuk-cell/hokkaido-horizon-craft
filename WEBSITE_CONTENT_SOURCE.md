## Booking And Pricing Strategy

Do not maintain detailed prices directly on the official website.

Hotels:

- Oriental Hotel Sapporo and Shinka Hotel Susukino should link to their Booking.com pages once links are available.
- Booking.com should carry real-time prices, room availability, cancellation policies, and detailed hotel rules.
- Website should present the hotel positioning, room type summary, location, gallery, and booking CTA.
- Hotel guests can ask about airport transfer discounts.

Airport transfers:

- Airport transfer discount applies to both hotels.
- Prices can be discussed in inquiry or shown only as a light "from" reference later if needed.

Private charters and day tours:

- Do not show fixed prices on the website.
- Pricing depends on route, time, vehicle type, season, and waiting time.
- Website CTA should invite guests to send date, route, number of passengers, luggage, and preferred pace.

Ski lessons and snow experiences:

- Do not show fixed lesson prices on the website yet.
- Pricing depends on date, resort, lesson type, language, level, equipment, transport, and group size.
- Website should focus on safety, multilingual support, family friendliness, and matching by level.

Restaurant:

- Oriental Hotel restaurant can accept group meals.
- It does not accept individual walk-in/personal meals.
