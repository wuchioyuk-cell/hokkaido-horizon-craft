## Suggested Website Placement

- Homepage: keep four-season Hokkaido nature as the main identity. Do not make the whole site snow-season themed.
- Services section: use one ski photo only for the `Snow Experiences` card.
- Snow Experiences page: use `12月11` powder/lesson images as the main visual set.
- Ski School page: use group-lesson images with instructors, preferably backs or goggles where faces are less identifiable.
- Social proof area: use close-up guest photos only after written or clear usage permission.

## Image Policy

- Prefer photos where faces are small, covered by goggles, turned away, or not central.
- Avoid using close-up children/guest portraits on the public website unless permission is confirmed.
- Crop images to emphasize snow, movement, forest, slopes, and instructor guidance rather than individual faces.
